**QOL Lite acts as a QOL Lock replacement to avoid 1% low issues. **YOU CANNOT HAVE ANY OF THE MODS INSTALLED ALREADY INCLUDED IN QOL Lite OR USE QOL LOCK WHILE USING QOL Lite OR IT WILL CRASH OR JUST NOT WORK!**

**QOL Lite MUST ALSO BE #1 PRIORITY IN YOUR MOD LOAD ORDER AT ALL TIMES!**
